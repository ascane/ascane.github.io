Chia-Man Hung

About Chia-Man

Chia-Man Hung is currently completing three masters programs. The first one is an MSc program in Data Science, in the department of Applied Mathematics at École Polytechnique. The second one is a joint program with École Polytechnique and an engineering program at Télécom ParisTech. The third one is Mathématiques, Vision, Apprentissage (Mathematics, Vision and Learning) as a visiting student at ENS Cachan, one of the top French Grandes Écoles (the top graduate engineering schools). Originally from Taiwan, she made her first courageous step at the age of seventeen when she decided to come to France to pursue her higher education, against her parents’ will. It is her deep belief that mathematics is the secret to understanding the world. She also enjoys learning new languages and she speaks Chinese, French and English fluently. She is a dreamer, a thinker, and a quick learner. With her combined experience in science and engineering, in an academic context and in industry, Chia-Man has a unique combination of skills that enables her to build things and to make the impossible into reality.

CV

Personal

She co-founded the mathematics student association "X-Poincaré" at École Polytechnique. They have organized a series of fun competitions, consisting of solving math riddles while jogging. Their goal is to share their excitement in math with others!

She is an amateur scuba-diver, with a level 2 diver certificate awarded after one and a half year of rigorous theoretical and practical training, allowing her to dive autonomously up to 20 meters without supervision of a monitor. She is also a student glider pilot at Aeronautic Association Coulommiers-Meaux, where she made lots of friends who are professional or amateur pilots!

She used to volunteer as an administrator and a webmaster of "Frankiz", a Polytechnique's website for students and alumni. She helped to create accounts and reply to all relevant questions.
