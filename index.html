About me

Chia-Man Hung is currently a MSc student in Data Science, in the department of Applied Mathematics at Ecole Polytechnique, an engineering student at Telecom ParisTech, and a visiting student in Mathematics, Vision, Learning (MVA) at ENS Cachan. Originally from Taiwan, she made her first courageous step at the age of seventeen when she decided to come to France to pursue her higher education, against her parentsâ€™ will. It is her deep belief that mathematics is the secret to understanding the world. She also enjoys learning new languages and she speaks Chinese, French and English fluently. She is a dreamer, a thinker, and a quick learner. With her combined experience in science and engineering, in academy and in industry, Chia-Man has a unique combination of skills that enables her to build things and to make impossible into reality.
