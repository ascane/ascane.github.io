About Chia-Man

Chia-Man Hung is currently completing three masters progams. The first one is an MSc program in Data Science, in the department of Applied Mathematics at Ecole Polytechnique. The second one is a joint program with Ecole Polytechnique and an engineering program at Telecom ParisTech. The third one is “Mathématiques, Vision, Apprentissage” (Mathematics, Vision and Learning) as a visiting student at ENS Cachan, one of the top French “Grandes Ecoles” (the top graduate engineering schools). Originally from Taiwan, she made her first courageous step at the age of seventeen when she decided to come to France to pursue her higher education, against her parents’ will. It is her deep belief that mathematics is the secret to understanding the world. She also enjoys learning new languages and she speaks Chinese, French and English fluently. She is a dreamer, a thinker, and a quick learner. With her combined experience in science and engineering, in an academic context and in industry, Chia-Man has a unique combination of skills that enables her to build things and to make the impossible into reality.
